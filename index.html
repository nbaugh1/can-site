#WestfieldCares x Canstruction

Canstruction® is an international 501c3 charity which hosts team competitions to design and build gigantic structures made entirely from full cans of food.


Westfield, in partnership with Canstruction, is hosting 14 events to benefit local food banks in our communities. Teams will fundraise, design and build their CAN-structures composed entirely of non-perishable food items. Westfield will host the structures as art exhibitions open to the public, and prizes will be awarded at each participating shopping center. At the close of the exhibition, all of the cans will be donated to locally selected food banks. Canstruction has raised millions of pounds of food in 170 participating cities and is one of the largest drives for food bank donations in the world.
